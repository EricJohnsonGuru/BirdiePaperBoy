Birdie Paperboy LLC is an online publishing company dedicated to exploring food, culture, travel, and the everyday moments that make life interesting. Our publications—All Ways Delicious, All The Noodles, and Eggs All Ways—deliver inspiration and reliable recipes rooted in culinary traditions from across the globe. From spicy street noodles in Bangkok to delicate egg dishes in Paris, our aim is to bring these experiences home, to every kitchen and every cook, no matter what their skill level.

At All Ways Delicious, we explore the best recipes from around the world—from Asia to Europe, the Americas, and beyond. We focus on providing approachable recipes that remain true to their roots while helping home cooks in the US bring international flavors into their own kitchens.

Eggs All Ways centers on the incredible versatility of eggs. We explore how this single ingredient can transform a dish, whether it's breakfast, dinner, or anything in between. You know the saying “you can't make an omelet without breaking any eggs”? Well, you'll be hard pressed to make souffle, meringue, frittata, Spanish tortilla, crêpes, cookies, cakes, and a whole lot of other things, too. We show all the ways that eggs are truly magical.

All The Noodles is all about the joy and comfort of noodles. Here we share our favorite noodle recipes from across Asia—from Chinese lo mein and Thai pad see ew to Japanese ramen and Vietnamese pho. We're passionate about these recipes because they showcase how something as humble as noodles can represent so much culture and tradition. And, well, mostly because we just love to eat all the noodles!

We write cookbooks, too! You can find most of our 40+ cookbooks on Amazon and wherever books are sold. Check out our bestsellers Ramen Obsession, Ramen for Beginners, Campfire Cuisine, The Baking Cookbook for Teens, The Homemade Ice Cream Recipe Book, and Deceptively Simple Desserts.

Together, our publications offer an invitation to learn, explore, and cook with confidence. At Birdie Paperboy LLC, we're not just sharing recipes; we're opening the door to the world's kitchens and helping you bring those flavors home.
